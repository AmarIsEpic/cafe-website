About Us

And where do you drink your morning coffee?

Welcome to Cafe Square, the newest and coziest coffee corner in Sanski Most, opened in 2024. Our philosophy is simple: to be that special place where your day begins with the perfect cup.

We're more than just a café - we're a community hub that comes alive with our famous coffee quiz, where people from all over the city compete for various prizes. The atmosphere is always friendly, warm, and buzzing with conversation.

Whether you're here for a quick espresso, meeting friends, or participating in one of our events, you'll find everyone ready to socialize over a good cup of coffee. At Cafe Square, every coffee break becomes a memorable moment.

General Knowledge Quiz

Every week we host a fun general knowledge quiz where people from all over Sanski Most compete for amazing prizes. Test your knowledge about history, science, pop culture, and more!

100+ Weekly Participants
Teams of 5 Top 3 Teams Win

Schedule & Rules:

  • When: Every Thursday at 7:00PM
  • Expectations: No quiz during summer break, holidays or scheduled breaks
  • Entry Fee: $3 per person
  • Team Size: Up to 5 people per team
  • 1st Prize: Liters of beer x weight of the heaviest team member (in kg)
  • 2nd & 3rd Prize: Case of beer
  • Note: No free drinks included with entry
